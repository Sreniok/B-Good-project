BESPOKE SKI & SNOWBOARD LESSONS FOR ALL :

WELCOME -Ski/Snowboard School We love skiing and snowboarding, but most of all we love teaching! We pride ourselves on teaching each person individually (even if you are in a class with friends and family). It is our job to adapt as quickly as possible to each person’s individual learning style.We are here to make your experience of skiing and snowboarding one to remember. We believe that by teaching simple techniques, you will build confidence and use less energy which will enable you to spend more time on the slopes enjoying the sun and snow.
